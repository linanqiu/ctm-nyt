the treasury plans to raise $ 175 million in new cash thursday by selling about $ 9.75 billion of 52-week bills and redeeming $ 9.58 billion of maturing bills . the bills will be dated oct. 26 and will mature oct. 25 , 1990 . they will be available in minimum denominations of $ 10,000 . bids must be received by 1 p.m. edt thursday at the treasury or at federal reserve banks or branches .
