enfield corp. said in toronto that it hopes to raise 56 million canadian dollars -lrb- us$ 47.7 million -rrb- through a rights offering to shareholders . under the offer , shareholders can purchase one enfield share at c$ 6.27 for each five shares held . in toronto stock exchange trading friday , enfield closed at c$ 6.75 , down 37.5 cents . the holding company said 0 the rights offering should reduce its c$ 171 million debt to `` more manageable levels '' before dec. 31 and allow it to finance future investments with equity capital . at last report , enfield had about 44.5 million shares outstanding .
