ford motor co. said 0 it is consolidating control of its asian operations under a new organization here that will be headed by w. wayne booker . ford asia-pacific will coordinate the activities of ford subsidiaries in japan , australia , taiwan and new zealand and work with ford business associates throughout the region . these functions are currently performed out of melbourne , australia , and at ford 's headquarters in dearborn , mich . mr. booker , executive director of ford 's latin america automotive operations since december 1988 , was named vice president of ford asia-pacific .
