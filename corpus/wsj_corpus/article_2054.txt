britain 's industrial production rose 1.5 % in august from july and was up 0.9 % from august 1988 , according to provisional data from the central statistical office . output in the energy sector , which can vary greatly with swings in the oil market , rose 3.8 % in august from may but was down 7.1 % from a year earlier . the latest figures compare with july 's 4.5 % month-to-month rise and 11.3 % year-to-year fall .
