private housing starts in japan were unchanged in september from a year earlier at 144,610 units , the construction ministry said 0 . the flat report followed a four-month string of declines . the down trend was partly the result of tighter credit sparked by a discount rate increase by the bank of japan in may . the central bank also unexpectedly raised the base rate by half a percentage point to 3.75 % oct. 11 as part of an inflation-fighting move that indirectly increases interest rates charged on new home construction loans .
