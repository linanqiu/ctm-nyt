the canadian government auctioned 750 million canadian dollars -lrb- us$ 637.5 million -rrb- of 9.25 % bonds due dec. 15 , 1994 . the average accepted yield bid was 9.617 % for a price equivalent of 98.523 . proceeds of the sale will be used to redeem c$ 675 million of government bonds maturing nov. 1 and for general government purposes .
