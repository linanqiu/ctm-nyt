carl e. pissocra , president and chief executive officer of bank one , dover , has been named regional president , a new post at the bank-holding company . mr. pissocra , 56 years old , will be responsible for the company 's 10 banks in the eastern region . dan j. hartwell , 41 , executive vice president of bank one , dover , was named president and chief executive of the dover bank , succeeding mr. pissocra . no one was named to succeed mr. hartwell .
