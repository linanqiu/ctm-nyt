french consumer prices rose 0.2 % in september from the previous month and were up 3.4 % from a year earlier , according to definitive figures from the national statistics institute . the state agency 's figures confirm previous estimates and leave the index at 178.9 , up from 178.5 in august and 173.1 a year earlier . the index is based on 1980 equaling 100 . a breakdown showed that food prices were the most active part of growth with a rise of 0.6 % . an official linked the gain essentially to higher prices for beef and pork . he said 0 summer drought problems that had hit several southern agricultural regions had stopped being a major source of price pressure in september .
