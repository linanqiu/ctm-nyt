coca-cola co . -lrb- atlanta -rrb- -- anton amon and george gourlay were elected vice presidents of this soft-drink company . mr. amon , 46 years old , is the company 's director of quality assurance ; most recently , he served as vice president , operations , for coca-cola enterprises . mr. gourlay , 48 , is manager for corporate manufacturing operations ; he was assistant vice president at the company .
