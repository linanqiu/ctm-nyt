papers : management and unions representing 2,400 employees at torstar corp. 's toronto star reached a tentative contract agreement friday , averting a strike by most employees of canada 's largest daily newspaper . members of the largest union , representing 1,700 workers , voted in favor of the pact yesterday . four other unions have yet to vote , but their leadership also recommended approval . the pact proposes a 2 1\/2-year contract with a raise of 8 % in the first year , 7 % in the second and 4 % for the final six months .
